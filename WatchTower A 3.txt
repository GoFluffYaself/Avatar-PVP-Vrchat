Transformers: WARLOCKED! is a dynamic and immersive Transformers-themed PvP community that brings players together for action-packed battles every weekend at 7 PM EST. Choose your allegiance and fight for dominance as one of three distinct factions: the heroic AUTOBOTS, the ruthless DECEPTICONS, or the independent and powerful WARLOCK, a full-PC mercenary faction.  

Our community is dedicated to providing an engaging and inclusive experience, with Quest compatibility ensuring that everyone can join in on the fun, regardless of their platform. Whether you're here for the intense PvP action, the camaraderie, or to immerse yourself in the Transformers universe, WARLOCKED is the perfect place to forge your legend.
